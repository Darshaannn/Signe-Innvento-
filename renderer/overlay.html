Welcome to SignBridge! 🚀

SignBridge provides an always-on-top real-time sign language overlay.

  • 🎤 Microphone Mode: Transcribes your physical voice.
  • 🖥️ System Audio Mode: Transcribes background applications (requires 'Stereo Mix' or 'VB-Cable' enabled in Windows Sound settings).
